About

GÄELLE is a Montreal-based pop and R&B artist defined by a strong and sensitive identity where vulnerability and empowerment intertwine. Her universe is built on intimate songwriting and a bold sonic aesthetic, carried by a powerful voice with an exceptional three-octave range — influenced by Ariana Grande, Beyoncé, and SZA — establishing her presence on the North American underground scene.

With rapid growth across social media, she is attracting an ever-growing audience well beyond Canada. After two singles released this year, she is currently preparing her second EP titled Sensitive Baddies, expected at the end of the summer.
